Dream Timer

Quietly build the time you spend on your dreams.

Dream Timer is a timer app for recording time spent on study, exercise, reading, side projects, work, and other goals. Small efforts stay visible, without turning your goals into pressure.

Story

For the days that feel like nothing got done.

Maybe you studied for ten minutes. Maybe you moved your body a little. Maybe you read just a few pages.

It may not feel like a big achievement, but it was time spent facing something that matters. Dream Timer helps you keep that time.

How it works

Recording starts with one timer.

1

Create a goal

Name what you want to build: study, exercise, reading, work, or a personal project.

2

Start the timer

Press start when you begin. No complex setup. No heavy management.

3

Save the record

Your time is added naturally. Add a note when you want to remember what you did.

Visualize

Small efforts become visible.

Daily records remain as a gentle heatmap. Busy days, quiet days, and small steps all stay there, so you can look back and notice that you kept going.

Badges

Time spent becomes a small reward.

Animal badges unlock as your recorded time grows: 50 hours, 100 hours, and 300 hours. The time you spend slowly takes shape.

Beyond 300 hours, more badges wait quietly. The app keeps a little mystery for later instead of showing everything at once.

Goals

A goal is not something that chases you. It is a place to return to.

Dream Timer does not push detailed completion rates or remaining time at you. Goals are there to remind you what you want to build.

Keep moving at your own pace. Your records stay quietly, and the time you have already spent becomes gentle proof.
